Our services

India today is an important source for International buyers. One cannot ignore India's capabilities in offering pharmaceutical testing equipment but sourcing them at the right price and ensuring that each one meets the International quality standards is not an easy task. In such a scenario an independent manufacturer and exporter with right sourcing capabilities is essential and with vast experience in this field is your perfect choice.

Our Complete Spectrum of Services include:

Order Execution

Orders are closely monitored at all stages from sampling till dispatch. The buyer is kept informed about the progress made in execution of the order.

Quality Assurance

Our aim is to deliver high quality and defect free materials and regular checks are carried out to monitor the production.

Mid-Production Check

To verify the initial problems if any are sorted out and the checking of the equipment is done accordingly.

Final Check

Before shipment, the consignment is finally checked, packed.

Dispatch Support

Shipping documents are checked to verify their conformity to buyers instructions and sent well in advance to the buyer after sales.
